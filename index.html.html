How It Works

Four steps.
One clean proposal.

No reformatting, no retyping. The engine handles categorization, structure, and delivery — you stay focused on the client.

1

Upload the vendor quote

Paste text or upload a PDF from any vendor. The engine validates and ingests the document automatically.

2

Items get categorized

Every line item is sorted into Base Parts, Add-Ons, or Accessories — preserving original names and pricing. Nothing invented, nothing estimated.

3

Packages are built

Choose single-price or tiered mode. In tiered mode, the engine assembles Good / Better / Best options automatically using only vendor-provided items.

4

Proposal + email sent

A formatted proposal PDF is generated with fillable labor fields, and a professional email is drafted — ready for you to review and send.

Features

Built for contractors who care about margins

This isn't a PDF parser. It's a standardization layer that makes every quote your company sends look like it came from a team of three.

  • 📋

    Strict data integrity

    The engine never invents items, modifies prices, or estimates missing costs. What you put in is what goes out — just organized.

  • 📦

    Good / Better / Best packaging

    Tiered proposals increase average ticket size by giving customers a decision, not a take-it-or-leave-it number.

  • ✍️

    Fillable internal cost sections

    Permitting, labor, electrical, and company add-ons are always included as blank fields — ready for your team to complete before sending.

  • 📧

    Ready-to-send email included

    A professional, no-emoji, client-facing email is generated alongside every proposal — branded to your company.
